About Me

Hello! I’m Austin — a passionate coder, tech enthusiast, and perpetual tinkerer. My curiosity knows no bounds: I love building websites, hacking GBA ROMs, and exploring all sorts of creative coding projects. With an ever-growing skill set that includes Java, Python, HTML/CSS, OOP, and Maven, I dive into each challenge ready to learn something new. I thrive on turning innovative ideas into functional software. From spinning up SaaS solutions in the cloud to experimenting with OBS for live streaming, I’m always looking for the next project that will push my boundaries. I believe in the power of open-source collaboration—sharing knowledge, debugging together, and celebrating breakthroughs with fellow coders keeps me inspired. Whether I’m crafting a brand-new web app, reverse-engineering a classic game, or fine-tuning a UI for a smoother user experience, my focus is on merging creativity with technology. Thanks for visiting my corner of the internet, and I look forward to connecting with like-minded enthusiasts to turn big ideas into reality!
